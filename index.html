REJECTION PROTOCOL — READ WHEN IT HURTS
THE TRUTH ABOUT REJECTION IN THIS INDUSTRY
Rejection is not information about your worth. It is information about the casting director's current need. You were not seen — you were filtered. The role you didn't get was already cast three weeks ago before auditions opened. Your job is not to book — your job is to be so undeniable that the one who does see you cannot say no. Every rejection is a rep. You are building your audition fitness. Amateurs treat rejection as verdict. Professionals treat rejection as volume. Keep the volume high. Keep the craft sharp. The booking comes to those still standing.

DAILY: Night Close in REFLECTION is non-negotiable. A day without Night Close is an unarchived day — it disappears from the record.
